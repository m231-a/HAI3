# Strategic Layer

**Audience:** Product Managers, Leadership, Stakeholders
**Focus:** Vision, market, and roadmap

## Overview

The Strategic Layer is where product vision is born and refined. This is the highest level of product planning, dealing with market positioning, competitive analysis, and long-term direction.

## Responsibilities

- Define product vision and value proposition
- Conduct market analysis and competitive research
- Prioritize features based on business value
- Create and maintain product roadmaps
- Align stakeholders on product direction

## Deliverables

From the Strategic Layer, you produce:

- **Market Analysis Report**: Competitive landscape, opportunities, threats
- **Product Vision Document**: Clear articulation of product goals and value
- **Prioritized Feature List**: Features ranked by business value and effort
- **Product Roadmap**: Timeline with milestones and releases

## Interface with Other Layers

**Consumes:**
- Business objectives and goals
- Market feedback and user research
- Revenue and growth targets

**Produces for Organizational Layer:**
- Product vision and strategy
- Feature priorities
- Resource allocation guidance

## AI Integration

AI can assist with strategic work by:
- Analyzing market data and trends
- Generating competitive analysis reports
- Suggesting prioritization frameworks
- Creating draft roadmaps from feature lists
- Identifying patterns in user feedback
